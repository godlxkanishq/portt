About

Story first.
Everything else follows.

I'm Kanishq — a video editor who treats every timeline like a score: pacing, silence and impact all placed on purpose.

My work lives where editing meets motion design — cinematic brand films, esports content, commercials and short-form that actually retains. I build hand-crafted motion systems instead of dropping in templates, sync every cut to the voice and the beat, and grade for a look that feels expensive. If it doesn't serve the story, it doesn't ship.
